Unleashing the Power of Artificial Intelligence

by Valen and Jenifer

Nov 10,2023

5 min read

T his new sewing machine, with a single needle, that can recognize up to 200 thousand types of seams, from jeans to chiffon and even leather.

If garment workshops had sewing machines with artificial intelligence, they could have optimized costs and delivery times even more.Its great advantage is that it optimizes the production capacity of the workshops, which until today operate at 50% and with the help of AI they can even reach 100%, since time (for changing the needle), man-hours and others," explained the representative. According to information on the official Jack brand website, this sewing machine has an AI chip, called Octopus Nine-Brain, with intelligent perception and real-time response to fabric changes.With its needle it identifies the fabrics and automatically sends instructions to the motor. Thanks to this rapid perception of fabric changes, this machine can adjust the rhythm of the synergy between the fabric feeding force and the needle penetration force. And so continuous sewing of different fabrics can also be done without adjusting the machine.
