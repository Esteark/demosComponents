The protocol involves commercial advice, new motorcycle delivery, and after-sales follow-up.

by Jenifer

Nov 10,2023

5 min read

I f you had owned a motorcycle before, you might have liked to have this protocol?.The first stage is the pre-sale, where the commercial advisor will have to guide those interested in purchasing a motorcycle in aspects such as: identification of the ideal motorcycle, according to the real needs of use and experience.Likewise, on the possession and qualities of road safety components in the vehicle, such as disc brakes and advanced braking systems, among which are ABS or CBS, or the automatic lighting system.

In addition, advisors must promote the participation of interested parties in training programs indicated by the ANSV.The second stage is the sale and delivery of the motorcycle. There, buyers will receive the safe and sustainable mobility guide built by ANDI within the framework of its 'Movemos Colombia' program.There, buyers will receive the safe and sustainable mobility guide built by ANDI within the framework of its 'Movemos Colombia' program.Then, upon delivery of the motorcycle, the advisor must review the possession of the Mandatory Traffic Accident Insurance (SOAT). In addition, he will suggest and verify that whoever picks up the motorcycle has a valid driving license and a helmet to move around.And finally, after-sales, whose purpose is for brands to implement monitoring processes for periodic inspections of motorcycles, and send information on road safety to their buyers.
